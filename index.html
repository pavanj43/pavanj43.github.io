About TimesAgencies



We are leading magazine distributors in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. We are in this trade for over three decades and distributing magazines all over AP & Telangana. We distribute magazines through New Agents, Non-conventional outlets, Super Markets and exclusive Book stores. We have extensive experience and resources to distribute magazines effectively. We have 84 Agents all over AP and Telangana including Hyderabad. We have offices and transshipment points at Hyderabad, Tirupati and Vijayawada for effective distribution of magazines. We have been distributing 38 magazines of 16 publishers and most of them on exclusive basis. Prominent among them are Magna Group, Business World, Dalal Street, The Hindu Magazines, Digit, Fortune India, Employment News, Civil Services Times, My Mobile and Chronicle Publications. We have unrivalled agency network in AP and Telangana and publications will reach nook and corner of these states. We deliver range of solutions to suit the needs of Publishers in most segments.
